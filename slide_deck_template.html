Investigation Overview¶

Ford GoBike is a public bike sharing system in USA. This presentation to discuss the bike usage pattern and user habit characteristics from 1.8 Milliom trip

Dataset Overview¶

The dataset is about monthly individual trip data from Jan 2018 to Dec 2018.

User Types and Thier Trip Duration¶

Subscribers are the majority of the user (about 86%). However Customer trip duration time seems to be longer than subscriber.

Hourly Usage for Subscribers and Customers¶

We can see the subscriber trip between 7 to 9 and 16 to 18 are the highst, this is because this is the time when people go and come back from the work.

Daily Usage for Subscribers and Customers¶

We can see that the subscriber have much less trip on weekends. I think becuase they use the bikes to go to thier work. and Customer has more trip on weekends and more time trip duration (as showen on first figure), I think customer use the bike for leisure and relaxing purposes. While the majority of subscribers use the bike to go to thier work and comeback.

Monthly Usage for Subscribers and Customers¶

Both subscribers and customers use the bike the most during summer months. The usage drop during winter months likely due to the weather
